The Undergraduate Government of Boston College (UGBC) is a student government created to enrich the lives of students attending Boston College. Its purpose is to serve on behalf of students' interests and to help cultivate an engaged and caring student community. We are promoting further development and progress of our organization, Boston College as a whole, and each of our fellow eagles as individuals.

Our mission

"cura" "personalis" "care" "of" "the" "whole" "person"

  • Academic Affairs

    Pursue academic initiatives and improvements as the Boston College undergraduate population grows and diversifies.

  • Student Life

    Effectively respond to undergraduate student life concerns to accommodate the needs and interests of students.

  • Inclusive Excellence

    Increase reach to marginalized communities on our campus through Inclusive Excellence (IE) programming and initiatives, further our collaborative efforts with faculty to increase transparency of and access to resources, and foster an overall inclusive culture within the broader Boston College family.

  • Experiential Equity

    Enhance collaborations with the Montserrat Coalition, the Pine Manor Institute, and Messina College to establish a fair, promising, and favorable undergraduate experience for students of all socioeconomic backgrounds.

  • Sustainability

    Further advocate for environmentally-conscious and sustainable practices throughout the Boston College community.

  • Organization Productivity

    Initiate appropriate adaptations within UGBC – regarding structure, role description, and task delegation – to increase productivity of our organization.
